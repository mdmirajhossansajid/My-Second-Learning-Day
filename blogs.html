My First Blog Post

visited to ham-ham-waterfall in Srimangal 2021

How to Get There

Take a bus from Srimangal to Ham Ham Waterfall.Hum Hum Waterfall (also called Ham Ham) in Bangladesh's Moulvibazar District is a popular, challenging trekking destination known for its beauty and adventure, located deep in the Razkandi forest near the Indian border. Visitors must trek through dense jungle, crossing streams and hills, often taking 2-4 hours each way, requiring proper gear, guides, and planning due to remote conditions, with the rainy season offering peak water flow but higher risk from leeches and slippery terrain

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Ham Ham Waterfall is during the dry season from November to April when the weather is pleasant and the trekking conditions are safer. Avoid the monsoon season (June to September) due to heavy rains, slippery trails, and increased leech activity.

What to Bring

  • Sturdy hiking shoes
  • Water and snacks
  • Rain gear (if visiting during the rainy season)
  • Insect repellent
  • Camera to capture the scenic beauty

visited to Jaflong in Sylhet 2022

How to Get There

Take a bus from Sylhet to Jaflong. Jaflong is a popular hill station in Bangladesh, known for its scenic beauty and lush greenery. The journey involves a scenic drive through mountainous terrain, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Best Time to Visit
